Cognizant and The Football Association
Cognizant has proudly been the Digital Transformation Partner of The Football Association (The FA) since 2017.  We are also a partner of The Emirates FA Cup, The Adobe Women’s FA Cup, The FA Community Shield and England Football.
Our work underpins the FA’s two-fold mission – to change the game of football to maximize impact and serve the game to deliver football to all. Through the solutions we provide for the FA, its players, coaches, managers and audiences, using cutting-edge technology and digital solutions, Cognizant is enabling football to enrich the lives of people and communities - from the grassroots up.
“Cognizant is strategically providing industry specialists to shape our ambition as well as designing, building and managing the technology programmes underpinning The FA.”
Craig Donald, CIO, The FA
